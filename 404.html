Ublock origin: click the uBlock Origin button in the toolbar > click Open the Dashboard > click 3rd-party > press F5 and search AFNIL > uncheck > press at the top Apply changes
 AdBlock Plus: click the AdBlock Plus button into the toolbar > click Options > click General > press button F5 on the keyboard and search AFNIL > press the button REMOVE
Adguard Adblocker: press the button Adguard AdBlocker in the toolbar > click Adguard Settings > click User filter >at the bottom of User filter > click clear (remove all filters)
